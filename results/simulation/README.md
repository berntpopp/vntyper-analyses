# Simulation Results

VNtyper 2 benchmarking results from MucOneUp-simulated MUC1-VNTR samples.

## Experiments

- **experiment1_dupC/**: 100 matched pairs, canonical dupC mutation (seeds 3000-3099)
- **experiment2_atypical/**: 100 matched pairs, 10 atypical frameshift types (seeds 4000-4099)
- **experiment3_coverage/**: Coverage titration at 75%, 50%, 25%, 12.5%, 6.25% of all 400 BAMs

## Key results

| Experiment | Sensitivity (95% CI) | Specificity | FN |
|------------|---------------------|-------------|-----|
| dupC | 94.0% (87.5-97.2%) | 100.0% | 6 |
| Atypical | 79.0% (70.0-85.8%) | 100.0% | 21 |

Zero false positives across all experiments and coverage levels.
